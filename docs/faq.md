## Will this create duplicate tasks?

Not always. The import report identifies tasks that were created, reused, or skipped.

## Can I import into an existing plan?

Yes. Use the target group and the same plan name. The app can match and reuse an existing plan by name.

## What does the Goal column do?

Goal can help with organising and follow-up context. Some goal-related follow-up may still need manual action after import.

## Who can use Import To Planner?

People with a Microsoft 365 work or school account and the required Planner access in their tenant.

## Is my CSV data stored by the app?

Imported Planner/task data is not persisted as application data. Limited operational logs or telemetry may still exist for reliability and support.

## What if my administrator blocks consent?

Ask your Microsoft 365 administrator to review and grant the required delegated permissions.
