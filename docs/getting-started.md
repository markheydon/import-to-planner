This page helps hosted users get ready, sign in, and start their first import.

## Prerequisites

Before you begin, make sure you have:

- A Microsoft 365 work or school account.
- Access to Microsoft Planner in your tenant.
- Membership in at least one Microsoft 365 group or team that can host a plan.
- A CSV file (or draft) ready for import.

## Open the hosted app

Use the hosted app URL:

- https://app.importplanner.app

If your organisation uses a custom entry link, use the link supplied by your administrator.

## Sign-in and consent expectations

When you open the app, you are asked to sign in with Microsoft.

You may also see a consent prompt. This allows the app to:

- Read your group and plan context so you can choose where to import.
- Create or update planner tasks as part of your confirmed import.

If your tenant requires administrator approval, you may be blocked until an administrator grants consent.

## Your first run path

1. Sign in.
2. Select a group.
3. Enter or select a plan name.
4. Upload your CSV file.
5. Review validation and preview results.
6. Confirm execution.
7. Review the final report.

Next step: continue to [CSV format](./csv-format) if you still need to prepare your file.
